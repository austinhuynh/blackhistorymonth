UC San Diego's 19th Annual Black History Month
Celebration and Scholarship Awards Ceremony

Friday, February 26th, 4-6PM
Register Today | Free | Virtual

Join us for UC San Diego's 19th Annual Black History Month Celebration and Scholarship Awards Ceremony. All community members are invited to come together virtually in recognition of student scholarship, while enjoying a live DJ performance, an inspiring keynote speech and more. The online celebration is free to register and attend. In lieu of a ticket purchase, attendees are invited to donate to the Black History Month Scholarship. To make your gift online, visit the UC San Diego Black History Month Crowdsurf page. All funds directed to the Black History Month Scholarship will go toward undergraduate scholarships, enabling talented and motivated UC San Diego students to achieve their academic and professional dreams.

This online event is part of a month-long series held at UC San Diego in celebration of Black History Month.
